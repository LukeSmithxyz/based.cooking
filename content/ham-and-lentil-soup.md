## Ingredients

- 2 cup Lentils
- 1/2 lb Ham, diced
- 1 Onion, chopped
- 1 Bay leaf
- 2 ribs Celery, chopped
- 1 clove Garlic, minced

## Directions

1. Combine all ingredients with 2 quarts water in the Crock Pot.
2. Cook on low, covered, 8 to 10 hours.
3. Adjust seasonings and serve.
