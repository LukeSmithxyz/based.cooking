# Coconut Oil Coffee

- ⏲ Prep time: 5 min
- 🍳 Cook time: 5 min
- 🍽 Servings: 2

## Ingredients

- 2 cups hot coffee
- 2 tablespoons coconut oil
- 2 tablespoons unsalted butter

## Directions

1. Blend coffee, coconut oil, and butter together in a blender until oil and butter are melted and coffee is frothy.
