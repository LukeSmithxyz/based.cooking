# Crab salad

- ⏲ Prep time: 10 min
- 🍳 Cook time: 30 min
- 🍽 Servings: 3

## Ingredients

- Сrab meat (300 grams)
- Egg (3)
- Rice (50 grams)
- Corn (1 can)
- Cucumber (1)
- Carrot (1)
- Onion (1)
- Mayonnaise (100 grams)

## Directions

1. Chop crab meat and all other ingredients
2. Combine and mix all ingredients
3. Add mayonnaise
4. Chill before serving
