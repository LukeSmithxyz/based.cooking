# Garlic Sauce

- ⏲️ Prep time: 5 min 

## Ingredients
- 7T mayonnaise 
- 6 to 7 cloves of garlic
- ½T ginger syrup
- 3T parsley
- 125ml sour cream

## Directions
1. Crush the garlic and chop the parsley.
2. Mix and keep in refrigirator.
