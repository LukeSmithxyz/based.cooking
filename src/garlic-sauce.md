# Garlic Sauce

- ⏲️ Prep time: 5 min 

## Ingredients

- 7 Tbsp mayonnaise 
- 6 to 7 cloves of garlic
- ½ Tbsp ginger syrup
- 3 Tbsp parsley
- 125ml sour cream

## Directions

1. Crush the garlic and chop the parsley.
2. Mix and keep in refrigirator.
