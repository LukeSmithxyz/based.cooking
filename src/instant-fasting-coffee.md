## Ingredients

- Ice Cold Water
- Apple Cider Vinegar
- Instant Coffee Powder

## Directions

1. Pour around 250ml or 8.45 fl.oz. ice cold water in a mug.
2. Add 1 tbsp instant coffee and 1 tsp apple cider vinegar.
3. Mix well. Sip slowly, and enjoy your fasting journey.
