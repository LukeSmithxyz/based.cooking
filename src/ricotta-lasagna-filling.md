## Ingredients

- Ricotta cheese 
- One large egg
- Parmesan cheese
- oregano (optional)

## Directions

1. mix the ricotta cheese together with a handfull of finely grated parmesan.
2. add the oregano and the egg and mix.
3. Add salt and pepper to taste.
